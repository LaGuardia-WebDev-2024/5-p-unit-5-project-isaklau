Dear... Choreographed and Performed by Rebecca Adamo, Mia Ganapolsky, Kayleigh Griffin, Isa Lau, Lucia Szell (LaGuardia Dance Junior Projects Showcase, 2024)

Dear... was choreographed and created as a result of a term long choreography course at LaGuardia High School. This piece was created to convey the message of never taking a mothers love and care for granted, and to acknowledge and appreciate our mothers.
